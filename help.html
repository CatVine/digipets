Help Guide

A quick reference guide to help keep you on track.

Choosing a pet

When starting a new game, you'll get the option of choosing a pet you'd like to care for! This will only affect the visual appearance of your pet.

Once a pet type has been chosen, this can only be changed by deleting your data and starting over from the Settings menu found in the main game screen.

Collecting Love

Once you've chosen a pet, you'll be taken to the main game screen where you'll see the pet you've chosen!

The game uses "Love" points as a form of currency. A pet will produce a set amount of love every 5 seconds, but only when you keep them well cared for. Keep them fed and watered using items from the shop to ensure a steady stream of Love from your pet.

You can also generate a set amount of Love by "petting" your pet (try clicking them)!

Caring for your Pet

Your pet has statistic bars in the upper left corner of the screen which track their level of Hunger and Thirst. These will visibly deplete over time until they hit 0. At this point, your pet will stop producing Love, while anything below 50% average health will still produce Love, just a lesser amount than average health above 50%.

To increase these statistics, you can buy Food and Water from the shop with Love to increase the Hunger and Thirst meters by 50% respectively. You can use these Items from your inventory.

If you've stopped generating Love and can't afford Care Items, try clicking your pet to get some more!

Buying Items

When opening the Shop menu from the bottom right button, you can choose from three different tabs of items.

Care: Food and Water for your pet, to increase their stats and keep them happy and healthy! Clothing: Cute items you can buy with Love and equip to your chosen pet! Decor: New backgrounds for your pet, to keep things fresh!

Food and Water are unlimited, but Clothing and Decor are a one-time purchase. Generate enough Love from your pet and collect all of them!

Equipping Items

Once you've bought an item, it will be stored in your "Items" menu, accessible from the bottom right button on the main game screen. By cycling through each tab, you can view all of the items you currently have.

For Care items, you can see the quantity of each Item you currently have on hand. For Clothing and Decor Items, you can see which items you currently have equipped. Choosing any other Item's "Equip" button will automatically replace the currently equipped item.

Changing Settings

The Settings menu is accessible from the top right button on the main game screen.

Inside, you'll find an option to turn on or off SFX and music. It's recommended to play with sound on for the full experience of the game, but this is off by default to provide users the option to choose their experience!

You can also delete your currently saved data from here. This will bring you back to the start screen and start the game afresh.

Inspirations

The sites that inspired this one!

  • Wajas

    A virtual pet site where you can raise, decorate and breed lots of colourful wolves. A staple of my childhood!

  • OMFGDOGS

    Just a very delightful site that has continued to give me joy over many years.

  • Plantgotchi

    A lovely little pixel art browser game where you raise a plant creature. Very inspired by Tamagotchi, which in turn inspired me!

  • Neocities

    Home to all manner of weird and wonderful sites, many of which capture the beauty of early 90s/2000s web design. Nostalgia to the max!

  • Duolingo

    Features a wonderful UI design, palette and brand language! Served as inspiration for Digipets' UI style.

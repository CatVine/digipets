About

A small introduction to the Digipets game and its developer!

The game developer

Hi, I'm Cat! I'm a Graduate Apprentice studying Software Development.

Much of my childhood was spent playing virtual pet games online and I've grown to have a very nostalgic love for them. It's one of my dreams to build a virtual pet site of my own and Digipets is my first attempt at pursuing that goal!

Digipets' development

Digipets is a university project built to showcase an understanding of Web Technologies at its core, but also to explore a fun project in the realm of my own interests!

It's been incredibly rewarding to work on and see it come to life, and I hope to expand on the ideas used here in the future to create a pet game that's even better!
